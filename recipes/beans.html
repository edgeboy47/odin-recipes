Black Beans

Black beans are a very tasty source of fiber, protein, and antioxidants.

Ingredients

  • Black Beans

Instructions

  1. Soak beans in water for at least two hours
  2. Drain beans after soaking, and add fresh water
  3. Cook beans in a pot for at least 30 minutes (You can also use a pressure cooker)
  4. Serve beans with a side of rice
