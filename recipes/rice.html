Rice

Brown rice is my favourite rice to eat, plus it is very nutritious and healthier than white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups brown rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 0.5 tbsp salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Add salt to water if desired
  3. When water starts to boil, add rice and cover
  4. Lower heat to a simmer and leave for 15-20 mins
  5. Take rice off of heat and fluff with a fork
